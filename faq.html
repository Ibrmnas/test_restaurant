FAQ

Some quick answers about delivery, halal, storage and payment.

Do you deliver outside Torino?

For now, delivery is in Torino only.

Are the marinades halal?

Yes — we use halal chicken.

How should I store and cook?

Keep between 0–4 °C and consume within 72 hours, or freeze immediately upon delivery.
Always cook chicken thoroughly to at least 75 °C, until the juices run clear.

Payment?

You can pay by Revolut or Satispay (details are sent in your confirmation email).

How to cook each marinade

Red Shish Tawook

• Best cooked in a pan or on the grill
• Medium-high heat
• Cook until juice run out, stir occasionally
• Can be eaten in a wrap or Lebanese bread with french fries, garlic, mayonnaise and ketchup sauces

Lebanese Fajita

• Best cooked in a pan
• Medium-high heat
• Cook until juice run out, stir occasionally
• You can add soy sauce while cooking
• You can add mozzarella cheese at the end of cooking and let it melt before serving
• Can be eaten in a tortilla bread or submarine bun with avocado and mayonnaise sauces

Lebanese Shawarma

• Best cooked in a pan
• Medium-high heat
• Cook until juice run out, stir occasionally
• Can be eaten in a Lebanese bread or tortilla bread with garlic sauce or hummus dip

Francisco

• Best cooked in a pan
• Medium-high heat
• Cook until juice run out, stir occasionally
• You can add mozzarella cheese at the end of cooking and let it melt before serving
• Can be eaten in a tortilla bread or submarine bun with mayonnaise or garlic sauces
